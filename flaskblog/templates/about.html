About Page

The application allows the logged in user to store text notes. Some notes may be marked as encrypted. Decryption should require a secret password.

The user can register and log in with a password.

The application allows you to share selected notes (unencrypted) with other users. Two solutions are allowed:

1) notes made available to selected users,

2) notes made available to the public.
